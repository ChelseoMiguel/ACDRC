GET STARTED

Africa Community Development and Research Center (ACDRC) is so far registered and vibrantly operational in six East and Southern African countries namely Zimbabwe (Southern Africa and overall headquarters), Zambia, Botswana, Malawi, Kenya (East Africa headquarters), Rwanda and Uganda. In Zimbabwe and Kenya ACDRC operates as a Non—profit Organization while in the other four countries it is registered as a private company with a very strong corporate social responsibility drive.

Our Vision, Mission and Objectives

Vision
The vision of Africa Community Development and Research Centre ACDRC is to transform Africa into a Hub of Knowledge for the modelling, training and delivery of cyclical community development programming, where research and community development innovations symbiotically feed into each oth- er as two sides of the same coin.
Mission
  • To technically support other organisations and beneficiaries of their programs with our specialized project or program management technical services in pursuit of a more vibrant civic society and informed communities;
  • To generate new knowledge from research and community development experience for evidence–based, effective and sustainable programming.
Objectives
  • To deliver expert consultancy services in the fields of public health, child protection, environmental protection, education, sustainable livelihoods, human rights and related components of community development.
  • To generate strategic information through action research for the improvement of ongoing and/or design of new community development initiatives.
